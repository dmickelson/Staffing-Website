Greater Boston's Premier Woman-owned Experts In Technology Staffing

Delivering the best talent, service and experience both the client and consultant

J Brown Partners is the preeminent woman-owned boutique IT staffing firm in the Greater Boston Area. For over 20 years we have been successfully partnering with clients and candidates by placing top quality candidates in all IT roles.

Our Unique Approach

With over two decades of experience, we take the time to truly understand our clients business, technology and staffing needs. We pride ourselves in build long-term, sustainable relationships by providing exceptional IT talent.

Our unique vetting and recruiting process by actual industry experts allows us to accurately qualify our candidates. We make sure we find the right technical fit, cultural fit and passion for the role (industry).

With a deep network of talented processionals, J Brown Partner can bring to bear top quality talent, quickly, efficiently and accurately. We have worked with clients in nearly every industry, and bring decades of experience to bear to focus on top quality candidates.

Our Consultant Support Program provides excellent resources, guidance and experience for our talent so they can focus on providing their expertise to our clients from day one.

Our goal is simple but meaningful: "Provide the best talent, service and experience to our clients and candidates"

What This Means

By deeply understanding our clients, vetting and qualifying candidates accurately, and making sure there is a fit in every area, we don't waste the time and money of everyone involved.

Not only does this make sure that there will be an immediate impact when candidates come on-board, but also that they remain part of the team long-term.
